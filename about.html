Our Story

Built by climbers who love teaching.

Conscious Climbing was started by a McCall-based couple who wanted to share the experience of climbing in a way that feels welcoming, calm, and confidence-forward.

Every outing is structured around your comfort level. Whether this is your first time touching rock or you are building technique for your next objective, we tailor each session to your goals.
